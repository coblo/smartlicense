Rights Profiles Demo:

Grants:


Always has to be selected. You may copy the work.

You may modify the licensed material and share your modified version.

You may make the material accessible to others in digital form.

You may sell your smart license “as is” to someone else.

Restrictions:


Always has to be selected. Patent and trademark rights are not licensed under this smart license.

You may not use the material for commercial purposes.

Obligations:

Indicate Adaptions
If you share an adapted version of the licensed material you have to indicate that your adaptation bases on the licensed material in a reasonable manner. You have to name the creator, copyright owner and licensor of the material according to the attribution obligation. If you share adaptations you must not imply that they are authorised or otherwise endorsed by the licensor.
Attribution
If the licensed material contains references to the creator or publisher or copyright notices you must not remove them.
